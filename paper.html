REVELATION FROM THE SEAM

"I LOOKED FOR YOU IN EVERY EMPTY HOLLER, AND FOUND ONLY THE PUREST EVIL STARING BACK."

I. THE JUDGE-PENITENT

TO UNDERSTAND THE FATALISM THAT PERMEATES THE LITERATURE OF THIS SOIL, ONE MUST RECKON WITH THE INESCAPABLE NATURE OF ITS JUDGMENT. THE APPALACHIAN SUBJECT EXISTS IN A "MALCOMFORT" CELL—A PUNISHMENT DESIGNED TO DISTORT THE HUMAN SHAPE UNTIL THE PRISONER CAN NEITHER STAND UP NOR LIE DOWN. POVERTY IS NOT MERELY A MATERIAL DEFICIT; IT IS AN EXISTENTIAL MARKING. TO BE RURAL IN AMERICA IS TO BE THE PUNCHLINE OF A JOKE YOU DID NOT WRITE.

II. THE RUPTURE OF DASEIN

IF POVERTY IS THE CONDITION, THEN THE LAND IS THE FOUNDATION OF BEING. TO BE HUMAN IS TO "DWELL". MOUNTAINTOP REMOVAL IS NOT MERELY ENVIRONMENTAL DESTRUCTION; IT IS AN ACT OF ONTOLOGICAL NEGATION. WHEN THE PHYSICAL LANDMARKS OF MEMORY ARE BLASTED INTO DUST, THE COMMUNITY IS UNMOORED. THE HOME BECOMES UNHEIMLICH—UN-HOMELY.

III. THE KNIGHT OF FAITH

BENEATH THE WEIGHT OF ENGINEERED RUIN, THERE IS A FEROCIOUS TRANSCENDENCE. RASH’S BECKY FUNCTIONS AS THE "KNIGHT OF FAITH," MAKING THE ABSURD MOVEMENT OF BELIEVING SHE WILL RECEIVE THE WORLD BACK DESPITE THE METH LABS. TO LOVE A RUINED PLACE IS THE BEGINNING OF UN-RUINING IT. THE MOUNTAIN REMAINS.
